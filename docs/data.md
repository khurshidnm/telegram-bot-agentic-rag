# System Access & User Roles

## Video Reference & Link
- Keeping | Нотариуслар учун махсус имконият! (https://youtu.be/o5YoV4HOdkc)
- Keeping | Аутсорс бухгалтерлар учун махсус (https://youtu.be/iqmPRbsBea0)

## Step-by-Step SOPs
- Notary Activation: Log in using a Jismoniy shaxs kaliti (Physical person key). Navigate to the "Sozlamalar" (Settings) menu, click the "Notarius" button, and confirm to unlock full accounting menus (Source: Keeping | Нотариуслар учун махсус имконият! - https://youtu.be/o5YoV4HOdkc).
- Outsource Accountant Registration: Click "Ro'yxatdan o'tish" and select "Buxgalter kabineti". Enter your passport and PINFL; data will populate automatically (Source: Keeping | Аутсорс бухгалтерлар учун махсус - https://youtu.be/iqmPRbsBea0).
- Workspace Activation: Use the SMS credentials to log in. You must sign the Ommaviy oferta (Public offer) with your personal ESI (Digital Signature) (Source: Keeping | Аутсорс бухгалтерлар учун махсус - https://youtu.be/iqmPRbsBea0).
- Firm Binding: Log in with the client firm's ESI, go to "Sozlamalar" -> "Buxgalter kabinetiga biriktirish", and enter the accountant's PINFL to assign permissions (Source: Keeping | Аутсорс бухгалтерлар учун махсус - https://youtu.be/iqmPRbsBea0).

## Troubleshooting Table
| Problem/Error | Root Cause | Specific Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Menus are inactive/hidden | Logged in as a standard physical person | Activate the "Notarius" toggle in the Sozlamalar menu (Source: Нотариуслар учун махсус имконият! - https://youtu.be/o5YoV4HOdkc). |
| Cabinet Inactive after Login | Public offer not signed | Sign the "ommaviy oferta" agreement using the personal ESI key (Source: Аутсорс бухгалтерлар учун махсус - https://youtu.be/iqmPRbsBea0). |
| Cannot Edit/Delete Data | Permissions restricted | Have the firm owner update access to full permissions in the firm's settings (Source: Аутсорс бухгалтерлар учун махсус - https://youtu.be/iqmPRbsBea0). |

## Critical Warnings
- If the PINFL is entered correctly, passport data will populate automatically. Warning: Verify the PINFL against official records if it fails (Source: Аутсорс бухгалтерлар учун махсус - https://youtu.be/iqmPRbsBea0).

# Bank & Cash Operations

## Video Reference & Link
- Банк ҳисоб рақамларидаги қолдиқларни киритиш (https://youtu.be/2WZ0s0N-DuY)

- Банк обороткаси юкланганда таҳлилда кўринмайдиган маълумотларга шаблон яратиш (https://youtu.be/Cai_5IleCiU)
- Сафар харажатлари ва корпоратив картадан сарфланган пуллар (https://youtu.be/OyuqhQdsOrQ)

## Step-by-Step SOPs
- Initial Balances: Go to "Pul mablag'lari" -> "Hisob raqamlar". Click "Qo'shish" (+). Set the date to December 31 of the previous year to set the opening balance (Source: Банк ҳисоб рақамларидаги қолдиқларни киритиш - https://youtu.be/2WZ0s0N-DuY).
- Account Binding: Label primary accounts as "Asosiy" and bind to 5110. Bind Plastic/Salary cards (e.g., starting with 23106) and Corporate Cards (e.g., 22620) to 5530. Bind USD/EUR accounts (code 840/978) to 5210 (Source: Ҳисоб рақамларга счёт бириктириш - https://youtu.be/MWW6YEvgKOs).
- Excel Import: Go to "Bank hisobotlari" -> "O'tkazilmagan". Download the Shablon, paste bank statement data exactly as formatted, and click "Fayl yuklash" (Source: Банк обороткани Эхселдан юклаш ҳамда проводка бериш - https://youtu.be/cbDCNAfOebo).
- Cash (Kassa) Reconciliation: When cash is withdrawn from the bank, it creates a pending entry. Go to the Kassa module and manually create a "Kirim" (Income) entry for that specific date to balance it (Source: Банк ва касса - https://youtu.be/_8p8RoF3cDw).
- Auto-Provodka: Go to "Ma'lumotlar" -> "Avtoporodka". Create templates based on Shaxsiy hisob raqam for automatic categorization of taxes and recurring fees (Source: Банк обороткаси юкланганда... - https://youtu.be/Cai_5IleCiU).
- Travel & Corporate Expenses: In "Avans hisoboti", use account 4220 for travel funds and 4290 for corporate cards. Bind the actual expenses to 9420 (Source: Сафар харажатлари... - https://youtu.be/OyuqhQdsOrQ).

## Troubleshooting Table
| Problem/Error | Root Cause | Specific Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Loan Showing as Asset | Positive value entered | Enter credit/loan balances as a negative number so they appear correctly in 6800 (Source: Банк ҳисоб рақамлари... - https://youtu.be/2WZ0s0N-DuY). |
| Kassa Balance is Red | One-sided entry only | Manually add a "Kirim" entry for the exact date of the bank withdrawal (Source: Банк ва касса - https://youtu.be/_8p8RoF3cDw). |
| Import Template Error | Mismatched columns | Use only the system-provided Excel template and do not alter column orders (Source: Банк обороткани Эхселдан юклаш - https://youtu.be/cbDCNAfOebo). |

## Critical Warnings
- If the account code does not trigger currency settings, do not proceed. Ensure the middle 3 digits identify the currency (e.g., 840) (Source: Ҳисоб рақамларга счёт бириктириш - https://youtu.be/MWW6YEvgKOs).

# Invoices & Electronic Documents

## Video Reference & Link

- Дебитор-кредитор қолдиқ (https://youtu.be/FSK5KTvQRl4)

## Step-by-Step SOPs
- Free-Form Documents: Go to "Savdo operatsiyalari" -> "Erkin shakldagi hujjatlar". Click "Yuborish", upload a .doc or .pdf, and assign signatories. Enable "Tartib bilan imzolash" for sequential signing (Source: Эркин шаклдаги ҳужжатларни юбориш имконияти - https://youtu.be/WZwMsVAbrNQ).
- Document Sharing: Copy the unique link at the top of a document to send via Telegram for remote parties to sign via QR Code (Source: Эркин шаклдаги ҳужжатларни юбориш - https://youtu.be/uuzNAPRDObE).
- Incoming Invoices Bulk Processing: Go to "Kiruvchi hisob-fakturalar". Click "Tahlil" (Analysis). Click a counterparty's total sum to assign a single Provodka (accounting entry) to all invoices simultaneously. Use the Red Arrow to copy logic to identical SKUs (Source: Ҳисоб-фактуралар билан ишлаш - https://youtu.be/7ALHtkW6WMY).
- Automatic Account Binding: During Analysis, enter the goods/services entries and click "Saqlash". The system auto-binds accounts to the invoices (Source: Кирувчи ҳисоб-фактураларга автоматик счёт бириктириш - https://youtu.be/WG9HpQWm_RA).
- Outgoing Invoices: Go to "Hisob-faktura" -> "Yuborish". Choose "Donalab yuborish" (Single) or "Pachkalab" (Batch) to create and send invoices mimicking the Didox/SoliqServis layout (Source: Ҳисоб-фактура юбориш - https://youtu.be/FCPiAgdkpl4).
- Initial Debtor/Creditor Balances: Go to "Ma'lumotlar" -> "Debitor-kreditor qoldiq". Enter opening balances for specific accounts and counterparties (Source: Дебитор-кредитор қолдиқ - https://youtu.be/FSK5KTvQRl4).

## Troubleshooting Table
| Problem/Error | Root Cause | Specific Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Remote Party Cannot Sign | No system login | Send them the unique document link. They can sign without full login access (Source: Эркин шаклдаги ҳужжатларни юбориш - https://youtu.be/uuzNAPRDObE). |
| System Slowdown | Sync period too large | Keep the sync period under 1 year; use 1-month or 1-quarter intervals (Source: Ҳисоб-фактуралар билан ишлаш - https://youtu.be/7ALHtkW6WMY). |
| VAT Information Missing | Bulk mode lacks status | Manually toggle the "VAT Payer" checkboxes during the bulk Analysis step (Source: Ҳисоб-фактуралар билан ишлаш - https://youtu.be/7ALHtkW6WMY). |

## Critical Warnings
- Warning: Fully signed free-form documents cannot be deleted; they are archived permanently (Source: Эркин шаклдаги ҳужжатларни юбориш имконияти - https://youtu.be/WZwMsVAbrNQ).
- If the business is a Turnover Tax Payer (Aylanma soliq), do not select the VAT (QX) option (Source: Кирувчи ҳисоб-фактураларга... - https://youtu.be/WG9HpQWm_RA).

# Import & Export Operations

## Video Reference & Link
- Импорт операциялари (https://youtu.be/aSvpZjQX6PM)
- Экспорт операциялари (https://youtu.be/0mo7m2PVVNE)

## Step-by-Step SOPs
- Import (GTD) Entry: Navigate to "Savdo operatsiyalari" -> "Eksport import" -> "Bojxona yuk deklaratsiyasi". Select the foreign importer. Enter the exchange rate, then click "Bojxona" (Customs) (Source: Импорт операциялари - https://youtu.be/aSvpZjQX6PM).
- Customs Valuation: Enter Customs Value (Bojxona qiymati), Fees, Penalties, and Duties. Enter VAT from GTD Column 48, Line 20. The system auto-distributes these costs into the final item price (Source: Импорт операциялари - https://youtu.be/aSvpZjQX6PM).
- Export Entry: Go to "Eksport import" -> "Eksport". Enter the current exchange rate and item price in UZS. Select "NDS-siz" (No VAT) (Source: Экспорт операциялари - https://youtu.be/0mo7m2PVVNE).

## Troubleshooting Table
| Problem/Error | Root Cause | Specific Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Foreign Importer Not Found | Missing from database | Add the entity manually in "Kontragent" using a dummy 9-digit code (Source: Импорт операциялари - https://youtu.be/aSvpZjQX6PM). |
| Export VAT Error | Incorrect tax status | Standard exports are VAT-exempt; ensure "NDS-siz" is explicitly selected (Source: Экспорт операциялари - https://youtu.be/0mo7m2PVVNE). |

## Critical Warnings
- Warning: VAT on imports is calculated based on the *Customs Value* (Bojxona qiymati), not the standard purchase price (Source: Импорт операциялари - https://youtu.be/aSvpZjQX6PM).

# Inventory (TMZ) Management

## Video Reference & Link
- ТМЗларни кирим қилиш (https://youtu.be/L5mO6CmGaDA)
- ТМЗларни чиқим қилиш (https://youtu.be/w8fnZZ1k6o0)
- ТМЗларни омбордан омборга ўлчов бирлиги бўйича кўчириш (https://youtu.be/T_4_3SCQkKY)

## Step-by-Step SOPs
- Inbound Logistics (Kirim): Go to "Aktivlar" -> "Kirim". Use "Hisob faktura asosida" for standard purchases and map to account 1010. Use "Ortiqcha aniqlangan" for inventory found during physical audits (Source: ТМЗларни кирим қилиш - https://youtu.be/L5mO6CmGaDA).
- Outbound Logistics (Chiqim): Use "Ishlab chiqarishga" to release materials to production (e.g., account 9420). Use the Strelka (Arrow) button to quickly copy expense accounts down the list (Source: ТМЗларни чиқим қилиш - https://youtu.be/w8fnZZ1k6o0).
- Benefits in Kind: To distribute inventory to employees, first create a "Natural Registry" in the HR module, then select it in the Inventory Outbound form (Source: ТМЗларни чиқим қилиш - https://youtu.be/w8fnZZ1k6o0).
- Transfers & Conversions: Use "Ko'chirish" to move items between warehouses (Ombor) or to convert units (e.g., converting 1 Ton to 1,000 Kg). The system auto-recalculates unit pricing (Source: ТМЗларни омбордан омборга... - https://youtu.be/T_4_3SCQkKY).

## Troubleshooting Table
| Problem/Error | Root Cause | Specific Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Cannot Output to Employee | No registry exists | Pre-build the employee distribution list in the HR "Natura Registr" first (Source: ТМЗларни чиқим қилиш - https://youtu.be/w8fnZZ1k6o0). |

## Critical Warnings
- Always check if construction materials must be returned from project sites (Account 9110) back to the warehouse (1010) via the "Davriy hisob" form at the end of reporting periods (Source: ТМЗларни кирим қилиш - https://youtu.be/L5mO6CmGaDA).

# Fixed Assets (Asosiy Vosita)

## Video Reference & Link
- Асосий восита (https://youtu.be/E4ejXp5SgWI)
- Асосий воситаларни комплектация қилиш (https://youtu.be/o7A5zypKO4w)

- Асосий воситаларни чиқим қилиш (https://youtu.be/oSLVhi04RmQ)

## Step-by-Step SOPs
- Asset Entry: Navigate to "Aktivlar" -> "Asosiy vositalar" -> "Kirim". Select items staged in account 0820 (from purchase invoices) and finalize them into account 0100 (Source: Асосий восита - https://youtu.be/E4ejXp5SgWI).
- Assembly (Komplektatsiya): Use this menu to group multiple staging components (e.g., monitor, CPU) from 0820 into a single fixed asset with a combined valuation (Source: Асосий воситаларни комплектация қилиш - https://youtu.be/o7A5zypKO4w).
- Depreciation (Eskirish): Go to "Asosiy vositalar eskirishi". Select the year and click "Eskirish" to batch-calculate depreciation for all assets (Source: Асосий воситаларга эскириш ҳисоблаш - https://youtu.be/pkVgeTmTk_s).
- Parameter Changes: Use the "O'zgartirish" tab to move assets between departments or change depreciation rates (Source: Асосий воситанинг параметрини ўзгартириш - https://youtu.be/Z_Ewvf4teY0).
- Asset Disposal (Chiqim): Choose the disposal type (Sale, Free Transfer, Investment). For "Sotish" (Sale), bind the asset to the respective outgoing electronic invoice (Source: Асосий воситаларни чиқим қилиш - https://youtu.be/oSLVhi04RmQ).

## Troubleshooting Table
| Problem/Error | Root Cause | Specific Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Asset not appearing for Kirim | Not posted to 0820 | Ensure the original purchase invoice has a provodka targeting account 0820 (Source: Асосий восита - https://youtu.be/E4ejXp5SgWI). |
| "Eskirish hisoblanmagan" Error | Depreciation missing | You must calculate depreciation up to the current month *before* changing parameters or disposing of an asset (Source: Асосий воситаларни чиқим қилиш - https://youtu.be/oSLVhi04RmQ). |

## Critical Warnings
- If an asset is used as an Investment Contribution and valued higher than its book value, the system will automatically log the differential as profit (Source: Асосий воситаларни чиқим қилиш - https://youtu.be/oSLVhi04RmQ).

# Master Glossary of Terms
- 0820: Staging account for fixed assets not yet in service.
- 1010: Standard accounting code for raw materials / inventory.
- Buyruq: Legal organizational order (e.g., hiring, termination).
- Debitorlik/Kreditorlik: Accounts Receivable (money owed to you) / Accounts Payable (money you owe).
- Erkin shakldagi hujjatlar: Free-form documents not strictly bound to standard tax invoice templates.
- ESI / Imzo: Elektron Raqamli Imzo (Electronic Digital Signature).
- Eskirish: Depreciation of fixed assets.
- GTD: Bojxona Yuk Deklaratsiyasi (Customs Cargo Declaration).
- Jismoniy shaxs kaliti: Digital signature key issued to a physical individual.
- JSHDS: Jismoniy Shaxslar Daromad Solig'i (Personal Income Tax / PIT).
- Kasallik varaqasi: Official sick leave certificate.
- Komplektatsiya: The process of assembling multiple inventory items or staging parts into a single Fixed Asset.
- Kontragent: Counterparty, vendor, or business partner.
- Kirim/Chiqim: Inbound (Income/Entry) / Outbound (Expense/Disposal).
- MXIK: Standardized classification code for products and services.
- Ommaviy Oferta: Public offer / Terms of service agreement.
- PINFL (JSHSHIR): Personal Identification Number for Physical Persons.
- Provodka: Double-entry accounting transaction or ledger record.
- QX (Qo'shimcha qiymat solig'i): Value Added Tax (VAT) / NDS.
- SOP: Standard Operating Procedure.
- Tabel: Employee attendance and timesheet.
- Tahlil: Analysis / Bulk processing tool.
- TMZ: Tovar-moddiy zaxiralar (Inventory / Raw Materials).
