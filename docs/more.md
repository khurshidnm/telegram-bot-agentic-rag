# System Access & Authorization

## Step-by-Step SOPs
- **Website Login:** Navigate to keeping.uz and click "Tizimga kirish". 
- **E-IMZO Authentication:** Authorization strictly requires an active E-IMZO (Elektron Raqamli Imzo) key. Upon the very first E-IMZO login, the system automatically creates a personal cabinet and binds official tax data; no separate registration form is needed. To log in on behalf of a business, the legal entity's E-IMZO associated with the organization's director must be used.
- **Alternative Login Setup:** After the initial setup via E-IMZO, users can bind their phone number in "Shaxsiy kabinet". The system will send an SMS with a login and password, allowing future access without the E-IMZO key for basic entry. However, tax syncing and legal document signing will still require the E-IMZO.
- **Notary Activation:** Notaries must log in using a Physical Person Key (Jismoniy shaxs kaliti). Navigate to the "Sozlamalar" (Settings) menu, click the "Notarius" toggle, and confirm to unlock full accounting menus.
- **Outsource Accountant Registration:** Click "Ro'yxatdan o'tish" and select "Buxgalter kabineti". Enter passport details and PINFL (Individual Identification Number) to auto-populate data. Log in with the SMS credentials and sign the Public Offer (Ommaviy oferta) using a personal ESI key.
- **Client Firm Binding for Accountants:** Log in using the client firm's ESI key, navigate to "Sozlamalar" -> "Buxgalter kabinetiga biriktirish", and enter the accountant's PINFL. Permissions can be set to full, specific functions, or read-only. Billing tiers allow managing up to 10 (Standard), 25 (Business), or 50 (Pro) firms.

## Troubleshooting Table
| Problem/Error | Root Cause | Specific Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Browser error during E-IMZO login** | E-IMZO app closed, missing plugins, or blocked permissions. | Ensure the E-IMZO application is running. Check that browser plugins are active, HTTPS is functioning, and browser notifications are allowed. |
| **Menus are inactive/hidden** | Logged in as a standard physical person. | Activate the "Notarius" toggle in the Settings menu, or ensure firm permissions are fully granted. |
| **Cannot manage multiple firms with one E-IMZO** | Missing authorization across different legal entities. | Ensure the E-IMZO is legally authorized for each respective firm. Each organization must be verified separately. |

## Critical Warnings
- **Warning:** The initial connection and full tax database integration strictly require an E-IMZO key. Login/password access is only a secondary convenience.
- **Warning:** If the PINFL is entered correctly during accountant registration, passport data will populate automatically. If it fails, verify the PINFL against official state records.

# Initial Setup & Tax Synchronization

## Step-by-Step SOPs
- **Primary Settings:** Immediately after the first login, navigate to "Sozlamalar" -> "Shaxsiy kabinet".
- **Tax Database Sync:** Click "Soliq bilan sinxronlash". This fetches organizational requisites, tax identifiers, and crucial data directly from the state tax system.
- **Director Information Update:** Within "Shaxsiy kabinet", locate the director information section and click "Yangilash" (Update) to refresh and confirm the latest active data from the tax database.
- **Telegram Notifications:** Access the "Telegram menyusi" to toggle desired alerts. A Telegram bot will send automated notifications regarding taxes, reports, and invoices.

## Troubleshooting Table
| Problem/Error | Root Cause | Specific Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Outdated Organizational Data** | Changes made in the official tax registry haven't reflected yet. | Click "Soliq bilan sinxronlash" multiple times to force the system to pull the most recent updates from the tax database. |
| **System slowdown or freeze during sync** | The selected date range is too extensive. | Keep synchronization periods to 1 year or less (preferably monthly or quarterly) to avoid system overloads. |

# Bank & Cash Operations

## Step-by-Step SOPs
- **Initial Bank Balances:** Navigate to "Pul mablag'lari" -> "Hisob raqamlar". Click "Qo'shish" (+). Set the date to December 31st of the previous year to establish the opening balance for the new year.
- **Account Binding:** Bind primary accounts (Asosiy) to 5110. Bind Plastic/Salary cards (starting with 23106) and Corporate Cards (starting with 22620) to 5530. Bind foreign currency accounts (e.g., USD code 840, EUR code 978) to 5210. Conversion accounts are bound to 5610.
- **Bank Excel Import:** For banks without direct integration, go to "Bank hisobotlari" -> "O'tkazilmagan". Download the exact system Template (Shablon), paste bank statement data (Date, Amount, Counterparty, Purpose), and upload the file.
- **Cash (Kassa) Reconciliation:** When cash is withdrawn from the bank, it creates a pending entry in the Kassa module. Manually create a "Kirim" (Income) entry for that specific date in the Kassa module to balance the books.
- **Auto-Provodka Templates:** Go to "Ma'lumotlar" -> "Avtoporodka". Create templates using the "Shaxsiy hisob raqam" (Personal Account Number) to automatically categorize recurring taxes and penalties.
- **Travel & Corporate Expenses:** In the "Avans hisoboti" (Advance Report) menu, select account 4220 for travel funds and 4290 for corporate cards. Bind the actual expenses (e.g., hotel, transport) to account 9420. Digital receipts can be uploaded for archiving.

## Troubleshooting Table
| Problem/Error | Root Cause | Specific Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Loan/Credit Showing as Asset** | Positive value entered. | Enter credit/loan balances as a negative number so they appear correctly in the 6800 liability accounts. |
| **Kassa Balance is Red (Negative)** | One-sided entry only. | Manually add a "Kirim" entry for the exact date of the bank withdrawal to reflect the cash arriving in the register. |
| **Import Template Error** | Mismatched columns. | Use only the system-provided Excel template. Do not change the order of columns like MFO, Account Number, or Purpose. |

## Critical Warnings
- **Warning:** If an account code does not trigger currency settings automatically, verify that the middle 3 digits of the account number correctly identify the foreign currency (e.g., 840 for USD).

# Invoices & Electronic Documents

## Step-by-Step SOPs
- **Free-Form Documents:** Navigate to "Savdo operatsiyalari" -> "Erkin shakldagi hujjatlar". Upload a .doc or .pdf file. Assign signatories. Enable "Tartib bilan imzolash" to enforce a specific sequential signing order.
- **Remote Document Sharing:** Copy the unique link at the top of a document to send via Telegram or email. Remote parties can sign via QR Code without requiring full system login access.
- **Incoming Invoices Bulk Processing:** Go to "Kiruvchi hisob-fakturalar". Click "Tahlil" (Analysis) to group hundreds of invoices by counterparty. Click a counterparty's total sum to assign a single accounting entry (Provodka) to all invoices simultaneously. Use the Red Arrow icon to copy accounting logic to identical MXIKs/SKUs.
- **Automatic Account Binding:** During the Analysis step, enter the goods/services entries and click "Saqlash". The system automatically binds accounts to all relevant invoices and posts them to the balance.
- **Outgoing Invoices:** Go to "Hisob-faktura" -> "Yuborish". Choose "Donalab yuborish" (Single) or "Pachkalab" (Batch). The interface mirrors the Didox/SoliqServis layout for standard compliance.
- **Initial Debtor/Creditor Balances:** Go to "Ma'lumotlar" -> "Debitor-kreditor qoldiq". Enter opening balances for specific accounts and counterparties for the previous year.

## Troubleshooting Table
| Problem/Error | Root Cause | Specific Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Missing Signature Field** | QR code not generated. | Ensure the file is saved after adding signatories to trigger the automated QR code placement. |
| **VAT Information Missing in Bulk** | Bulk mode lacks tax status fetch. | Manually toggle the "VAT Payer" checkboxes during the bulk Analysis step to apply VAT to the entries. |

## Critical Warnings
- **Warning:** Fully signed free-form documents cannot be deleted; they are archived permanently in the system.
- **Warning:** If the business is a Turnover Tax Payer (Aylanma soliq), do not select the VAT (QX) option during invoice processing.

# Import & Export Operations

## Step-by-Step SOPs
- **Import (GTD) Entry:** Navigate to "Savdo operatsiyalari" -> "Eksport import" -> "Bojxona yuk deklaratsiyasi". Enter the declaration date, number, currency, and exchange rate.
- **Foreign Partner Setup:** If the foreign importer is not in the system, add them manually in the "Kontragent" menu using a dummy 9-digit unique code.
- **Customs Valuation & Calculation:** Click the "Bojxona" (Customs) button. Enter values for Customs Value (Bojxona qiymati), Fees (Yig'im), Penalties (Jarima), and Customs Duty (Boj). Enter the VAT (QX) exactly as it appears in GTD Column 48, Line 20. The system automatically distributes these costs into the final cost of the goods.
- **Export Entry:** Navigate to "Eksport import" -> "Eksport". Enter the current exchange rate and the item price in UZS. The system displays the equivalent value in foreign currency. Select "NDS-siz" (No VAT) for the tax status.

## Troubleshooting Table
| Problem/Error | Root Cause | Specific Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Incorrect Debtor Balance on Export** | Currency conversion mismatch. | Verify the exchange rate entered manually matches the official rate for the exact date of the export declaration. |
| **Missing Bank Link for Imports** | Incorrect account code. | Ensure customs payments are coded to account 4890 in bank transactions so they auto-sync with the GTD entries. |

## Critical Warnings
- **Warning:** VAT on imports is calculated based on the Customs Value (Bojxona qiymati) assigned by customs officials, not the standard purchase price.
- **Warning:** Standard exports are VAT-exempt; ensure "NDS-siz" is explicitly selected to avoid balance errors.

# Inventory (TMZ) & Warehouse Management

## Step-by-Step SOPs
- **Warehouse Creation:** Before tracking inventory, navigate to "Ombor", click "Qo'shish", specify the warehouse name and the financially responsible person (moddiy javobgar shaxs), and save.
- **Inbound Logistics (Kirim):** Go to "Aktivlar" -> "Kirim". Use "Hisob faktura asosida" for standard purchases and map to account 1010. Use "Ulush sifatida" for founder contributions (which generates a digital Acceptance Act). Use "Ortiqcha aniqlangan" for excess inventory found during physical audits. Use "Davriy hisob" to return unused construction materials from project sites (Account 9110) back to the warehouse (1010).
- **Outbound Logistics (Chiqim):** Use "Ishlab chiqarishga" to release materials to production (e.g., account 9420). 
- **Benefits in Kind (Natural Form):** To distribute inventory as salary/benefits to employees, first create a "Natura Registr" in the HR module, then select that registry in the Inventory Outbound form.
- **Transfers & Conversions:** Use "Ko'chirish" to move items between warehouses or change accounting codes (e.g., 1010 to 1020). Use the conversion tool to change bulk units to smaller units (e.g., 1 Ton to 1,000 Kg); the system auto-recalculates the unit pricing.
- **MXIK Automation:** Bind MXIK codes to goods. For future invoices containing these codes, the system automatically recognizes the items and applies the correct provodkas.

## Troubleshooting Table
| Problem/Error | Root Cause | Specific Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cannot Output Inventory to Employee** | No registry exists. | Pre-build the employee distribution list in the HR "Natura Registr" before attempting the inventory outbound process. |

## Critical Warnings
- **Warning:** It is highly recommended to create your Ombor (Warehouse) immediately after registration. Tracking inbound/outbound stock without an established warehouse will severely complicate accounting.

# Fixed Assets (Asosiy Vosita)

## Step-by-Step SOPs
- **Asset Entry (Kirim):** Navigate to "Aktivlar" -> "Asosiy vositalar" -> "Kirim". Select items staged in account 0820 (which arrived via purchase invoices) and finalize them by assigning them to a department and account 0100.
- **Assembly (Komplektatsiya):** Use this menu to group multiple staging components (e.g., a monitor, CPU, and keyboard from 0820) into a single fixed asset (e.g., "Computer") with a combined valuation.
- **Depreciation (Eskirish):** Go to "Asosiy vositalar eskirishi". Select the year and click "Eskirish" to batch-calculate depreciation for all assets simultaneously. 
- **Parameter Changes:** Use the "O'zgartirish" tab to move assets between departments or adjust their depreciation rates.
- **Asset Disposal (Chiqim):** Choose the disposal type. For "Sotish" (Sale), bind the asset to the respective outgoing electronic invoice. For "Ustav kapitaliga ulush" (Investment), enter the recipient firm and agreed value. For "Ta'sischi chiqishi", process founder withdrawals.

## Troubleshooting Table
| Problem/Error | Root Cause | Specific Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Asset not appearing for Registration/Kirim** | Not posted to 0820. | Ensure the original purchase invoice has an accounting entry (provodka) specifically targeting staging account 0820. |
| **"Eskirish hisoblanmagan" Error on Disposal/Edit** | Depreciation missing for current period. | You must calculate depreciation up to the current month before changing parameters or disposing of an asset. If disposing after the 15th, calculate the current month's depreciation first. |
| **Residual Value Mismatch on Sale** | Incorrect sale price entered. | In the "Sotish" form, ensure the manual sale price entered matches the total on the linked tax invoice exactly. |

## Critical Warnings
- **Warning:** If an asset is used as an Investment Contribution and valued higher than its current book value, the system will automatically log the differential as profit.

# HR, Payroll & Personnel

## Step-by-Step SOPs
- **Citizen Registration:** In "Xodimlar" -> "Ma'lumotlar", click add and enter the citizen's Passport and PINFL. The system syncs with the national database to pull demographic data automatically. Enter a phone number to allow Telegram bot integration for payslips.
- **Hiring Order (Buyruq):** Go to "Ishga qabul qilish". Select the registered citizen, assign a department, work schedule, position, and salary. Click "Hujjat yaratish" to generate the official legal hiring order.
- **Parameter Adjustments:** Use "O'zgartirish" to promote employees or adjust salaries based on new organizational orders.
- **Additions & Deductions:** Use "Qo'shimchalar/Ushlanmalar" for one-time bonuses or recurring deductions (such as Alimony or Loans). Set end dates to automatically stop recurring deductions.
- **Leave & Sick Pay:** In "Buyruqlar", enter "Kasallik varaqasi" (Sick leave) details including the certificate number. The system applies 60%, 80%, or 100% compensation automatically based on the employee's tenure (staj). Use "O'z hisobidan" for unpaid leave.
- **Payroll (Tabel & Hisoblash):** The system generates the Tabel (Attendance sheet) automatically based on hiring orders and leave entries. Once the Tabel is verified and saved, navigate to "Ish haqqi hisoblash" and select the month to calculate net pay and taxes in one click.

## Troubleshooting Table
| Problem/Error | Root Cause | Specific Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **"Xodim topilmadi" (Citizen Not Found)** | Expired Passport. | The system cannot fetch data for expired passports from the national database. A valid, unexpired document is strictly required. |

## Critical Warnings
- **Warning:** Days marked as "Sick" or "Vacation" via HR orders are automatically excluded from working hours in the Tabel. Do not manually adjust the Tabel for these days.

# Tax Accounting & Other Revenue

## Step-by-Step SOPs
- **Entering Tax Balances:** Go to "Ma'lumotlar" -> "Soliq hisobi". Select December 31st of the previous year, choose "Qoldiq" (Balance), and enter existing tax liabilities and assets. 
- **Ongoing Tax Calculations:** For non-automated taxes (Land, Property, Water, Agriculture), select the current month and click "Hisoblash" (Calculate) to manually enter calculated tax liabilities.
- **Form 111 (JSHDS - Personal Income Tax):** Navigate to the Tax menu. Form 111 is pre-populated based on payroll and rental data. If adjustments are needed, download the Excel Template, fill in the "Imtiyoz" (Privilege) columns utilizing proper codes, and upload the file to override.
- **Non-Invoice Revenue:** Go to "Aktivlar" -> "Boshqa daromadlar". Manually enter cash sales or discrepancies where no electronic invoice was generated. This ensures the digital ledger matches official state tax reports.

## Troubleshooting Table
| Problem/Error | Root Cause | Specific Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Taxes missing from manual balance entry** | Taxes are Auto-Calculated. | Payroll taxes like INPS, JSHDS, and Social Tax are excluded from the manual entry list because they are calculated automatically by the payroll module. |
| **Form 111 Submission Rejected** | Incorrect Privilege Codes. | Ensure codes used in column 15 of the Excel override match the exact dropdown options provided in the system's "Imtiyoz" sub-menu. |
| **Social Tax Discrepancy** | Reporting mismatch. | If there are specific social tax privileges, they must be manually selected in the first appendix (1-ilova) of the Social Tax menu. |

## Critical Warnings
- **Warning:** In Form 111, the mandatory 1% pension contribution (line 011) is strictly validated against the taxable base. Do not miscalculate this cell in the Excel override, or the system will reject the form.

# Master Glossary of Terms
- **0820:** Staging account for fixed assets not yet in service.
- **1010:** Standard accounting code for raw materials / inventory.
- **Buyruq:** Legal organizational order (e.g., hiring, termination, leave).
- **Debitorlik/Kreditorlik:** Accounts Receivable (money owed to you) / Accounts Payable (money you owe).
- **E-IMZO / ESI / Imzo:** Elektron Raqamli Imzo (Electronic Digital Signature) required for legal authorization and signing contracts.
- **Erkin shakldagi hujjatlar:** Free-form documents (.doc/.pdf) not strictly bound to standard tax invoice templates.
- **Eskirish:** Depreciation of fixed assets.
- **GTD:** Bojxona Yuk Deklaratsiyasi (Customs Cargo Declaration).
- **INN/INPS:** Taxpayer Identification Number / Personal Pension Account Number.
- **Jismoniy shaxs kaliti:** Digital signature key issued to a physical individual (used by Notaries).
- **JSHDS:** Jismoniy Shaxslar Daromad Solig'i (Personal Income Tax / PIT).
- **Kasallik varaqasi:** Official sick leave certificate.
- **Kirim / Chiqim:** Inbound (Income/Entry) / Outbound (Expense/Disposal).
- **Komplektatsiya:** The process of assembling multiple inventory items or staging parts into a single Fixed Asset.
- **Kontragent:** Counterparty, vendor, foreign importer, or business partner.
- **MXIK:** Standardized classification code for identifying specific products and services on electronic invoices.
- **Ombor:** The Warehouse module utilized to track inventory and stock flow.
- **Ommaviy Oferta:** Public offer / Terms of service agreement.
- **PINFL (JSHSHIR):** Personal Identification Number for Physical Persons.
- **Provodka:** Double-entry accounting transaction or ledger record automatically or manually generated.
- **QX (Qo'shimcha qiymat solig'i) / NDS:** Value Added Tax (VAT).
- **Shaxsiy kabinet:** The Personal Cabinet dashboard where central settings, syncs, and profile adjustments are made.
- **Soliq bilan sinxronlash:** The system action that actively synchronizes with the state tax database to download organizational updates and invoices.
- **SOP:** Standard Operating Procedure.
- **Tabel:** Employee attendance and timesheet.
- **Tahlil:** Analysis / Bulk processing tool for invoices and bank statements.
- **TMZ:** Tovar-moddiy zaxiralar (Inventory / Raw Materials).
